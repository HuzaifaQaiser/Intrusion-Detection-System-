Intrusion Detection System

Our Intrusion Detection System (IDS) project aims to enhance cybersecurity measures through cutting-edge technology. Leveraging advanced algorithms and machine learning models, our system monitors network traffic and system activities to detect and respond to potential security breaches in real-time.

Real-time Monitoring: Our IDS continuously analyzes network traffic and system logs to identify suspicious activities as they occur.

Anomaly Detection: By employing machine learning algorithms, our system can detect deviations from normal behavior patterns, allowing for the early detection of intrusions.
